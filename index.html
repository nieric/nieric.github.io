I am Eric Nilsson, born 1997 in Sweden, currently residing in Gothenburg, Sweden. You can find my cv here.


What I do

I am a currently a Ph.D. student at Chalmers University of Technology (Aug 2022 - ~Jun 2026) under the supervision of Prof. Ulf Gran. My research is focused on various aspects of novel electron transport. This includes hydrodynamic and tomographic transport in 2D materials, as well as strange metal phase in high-temperature superconductors. I have modeled electrons in 2D using kinetic theory, and investigated plasmons in strange metals using holography ("AdS/CMT"), where one solves a gravitaional problem in higher dimension.
